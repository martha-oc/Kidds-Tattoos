About me

Hi! My name is Alicia Kidd and I am a tattoo apprentice based in Drogheda, Co. Louth. I am now in year 2 of my apprenticeship at Loud Ink - located on Meat Market Lane. I attended the Limerick School of Art and Design where I specialised in Painting, and graduated with a Bachelors degree in Fine Art in 2022. At present I am leaning towards black and grey realism and illustrative realism in my fake skin tattoo practice. You can catch up with my work here or on my instagram page @kiddtattoos. Thanks for stopping by!
